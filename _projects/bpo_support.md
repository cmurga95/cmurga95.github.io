## Summary

During my time at Confie I provided support to the BPO Department, which consisted of over 10 divisions.

My responsabilities consisted of:

- Validation of data
- SQL Statements
- Database - Tableau connections
- Design of Tableau Dashboards
- Maintainance of the above

## Objective

To provide practical and reliable visibility to stakeholders though the development and maintainance of Tableau Dashboards.

## Tools used

- SQL
- Tableau

## Outcome

One Dashboard per division, and additionally one dashboard to visualize a summary of KPIs reached for each division.
